🧶 Cricket's Crochet Toolkit

three little helpers, one app

📲 Put it on your Home Screen

Install once and the whole toolkit works offline, like a real app.

  1. Open this page in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button → Add to Home Screen → Add.
  3. Open it from the new Cricket's icon.

💡 Good to know

All three tools share the same yarn list, saved on this device only — no account, nothing uploaded. Make a backup now and then in Stash Manager → Data → Back up now.
